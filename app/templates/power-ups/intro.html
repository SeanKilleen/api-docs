Power-Ups Introduction

Power-Ups are powerful independent modules that allow developers to modify the Trello experience while still maintaining member control. Power-Ups represent the ability to enrich the experience of members by modifying and improving their interactions with Boards and Cards. Power-Ups are currently only available to members as part of Business Class or Enterprise.

Our current set of Power-Ups has some great examples of what the platform can do. Learn more about our GitHub Power-Up, or our Salesforce Power-Up.

Power-Up Samples

Trello has built two sample Power-Ups that you can use to get started developing right away. These sample demonstrate of the capabilities of the platform and should help you to get started.

Getting Started

Interested in building your own Power-Up? First, clone/download our samples above, or get started by building your own manifest and index connector. Once you've got something ready and hosted via HTTPS, there are two ways to hook up your Power-Up.

  • Local Development Only

    Visit https://trello.com/power-up-preview and enter an HTTPS-based manifest URL. This will allow you to see and enable your Power-Up only for your current browser and member. Other members of your team won't be able to see your Power-Up, and you will see your Power-Up on all boards belonging to BC or Enterprise Teams that you have access to.

  • Team Development

    Click the button below to request Sandbox access to the Platform. Sandbox access is available for Team Admins, and allows you to attach a Power-Up to your own Team for development and private usage. We'll need your team name and HTTPS based manifest to get you setup.
